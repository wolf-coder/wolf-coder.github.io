The story

  • While I was taking my Machine Learning specialty at Holberton School, I made it a point to read two essential books:
    • Mathematics for Machine Learning
    • Machine Learning: A Probabilistic Perspective
  • By doing so, I was able to move beyond the mindset of just learning an algorithm and appreciate the mathematical principles behind the algorithms.
  • This page will contain a graphical front-end (org-roam-ui) linking all my ML projects to these books
  • The first ones who will benefit from this work are Holberton School students.
